# Medical-AI liability map (HK / GBA / EU) — depth asset starter

**Status:** draft skeleton  
**Audience:** CMEL / LITE / life-sciences counsel  
**Sources:** public only

## Question

Who is liable when a Hong Kong hospital deploys an AI clinical decision-support system that contributes to patient harm: treating clinician, hospital, software vendor, model deployer, or data provider?

## Why it matters
- Digital health M&A diligence increasingly turns on model provenance, clinical validation, and contractual risk allocation.
- GBA cross-border deployment creates data-export and licensing asymmetries.
- Soft-law vs product-regulation vs professional discipline remains unsettled in HK.

## Decision tree (v0)
1. Is the system a medical device / SaMD under applicable classification?
2. Was there a clinician in the loop with meaningful override?
3. Contractual indemnity / limitation clauses between hospital and vendor?
4. Was training/validation data lawfully obtained (consent / secondary use)?
5. Cross-border: was personal health data transferred under PIPL / PDPO constraints?
